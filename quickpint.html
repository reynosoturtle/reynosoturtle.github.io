About

Have you ever thought of going a quick drink (or what we like to call, a Quick Pint) after a long day of work? Last minute plans like that usually a few considerations to factor in. Do I have work the next day? If so, is it *worth it*?

Still can't decide?

In Quick Pint, we give you options. But wait! Having more options just makes it even harder! We give you options that are automatically sorted from the nearest to the furthest. If that is not enough, there is also the option to sort by the price of a pint dictated by the owner.

I can't remember which bar I went to last time!

Quick Pint gives you the ability to favourite bars which allows for easier access in the future. Now get drinking!
