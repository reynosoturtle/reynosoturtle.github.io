About

Ah, my very first project in GA. When the days were simpler and before stress took control over my life.
In this project, we were tasked to create a browser based game with Javascript. This project was truly enjoyable because I was recreating two of my favourite minigames from Super Mario Party.

Game 1 - Looking for Love

Look for the heart! Don't let the colour and other suits confuse you.
Controls:
Player 1: W (Up) A (Down) S (Left) D (Right)
Player 2: ↑ (Up) ↓ (Down) ← (Left) → (Right)

Game 2 - Absent Minded

Figure out who isn't there!
Controls:
Player 1: W (Select) A (Left) D (Right)
Player 2: ← (Left) → (Right) ↑ (Select)
