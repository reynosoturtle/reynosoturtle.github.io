About

This was a design project I did forLKCNHM@NUS. I was tasked to promote the museum and increase their visit count. When I visited the museum, I was completely immersed when I entered two specific rooms. One was themed after the forest, it was lit by green lights and you could hear sounds from animals you would find in a forest. The other was themed after the ocean, it was lit by blue lights and you could hear sounds from the ocean such as the waves and more distinct sounds like whales.

Concept

I found that people wouldn't find the value of visiting the museum until they actually do. The issue is convincing them to do so as NUS is practically a country on its own, I would not be surprised if they have their own currency. The idea is to "bring the museum to them", to entice them into making that inconvenient trip to get the full experience.

Execution

To achieve this, I based my ambient ad in locations that could replicate the environment. For example, I chose an enclosed escalator area for the forest theme. As you ascend or descend. you will hear different animals that inhabit the respective heights.
