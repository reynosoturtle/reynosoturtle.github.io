About

This is an ongoing project I took up for Loue Bicycles. I was tasked to redesign and develop their website. With the new skills I picked up through GA, I am now able to take the project a few steps further than originally planned.

Problem(s)

Their current website offers, for the lack of a better term, shady transactions. It required clients to pay a deposit for the product they purchased. Afterwards, the products will arrive at the store then the clients would have to drop by there to collect the item and also pay the remaining balance in cash. Booking appointments also required a lot of manual work to be done which I would like to automate as well.
