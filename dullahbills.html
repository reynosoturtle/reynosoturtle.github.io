About

There are many ways to split the bill. While most choose to go Dutch, there will always be some who end up at the losing end. Over time, this could cause unwanted frictions in groups as the numbers cumulate. While there is always the option of splitting it by the item but with taxes involved, it is just too much of a hassle. Dullah Bills gets the hassle out of the equation.

Split bills with ease

What sets Dullah Bills apart from the existing apps out there like Splitwise is the implementation of OCR technology. Although the technology goes way back, there is no perfect one out there. This is also another issue that surfaced after experimenting with it. Our simple solution is to have it give us the first layer of input, then give the user the chance to vet and rectify the inaccurarcate values, if necessary.

Keep track of who is paying for what

We enforce the payers and payees to be registered to the app. This is so that they are all able to see the bills they are involved with, regardless of being a payer or payee. This also opens up opportunities for us to implement cooler features such as payment systems like PayLah! and keeping track of the status of payments.
